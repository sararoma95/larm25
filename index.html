Laura Rojas - Architect – Interior Design

About Me

I’m Laura Rojas, an Architectural and Interior Design professional with over four years of experience in creating precise technical drawings, BIM coordination, and design documentation for residential, workplace, and hospitality projects. My background blends strong technical expertise in AutoCAD, Revit (Autodesk Certified 2024), and Adobe Creative Suite with a passion for thoughtful, functional, and detail-oriented design.

Having worked with multidisciplinary teams in both Colombia and Canada, I’ve developed a deep appreciation for collaboration, intercultural communication, and efficiency in project delivery. I’m particularly skilled at optimizing BIM workflows, ensuring accuracy in construction documentation, and bridging the gap between design intent and execution.

Currently based in Vancouver, BC, I’m pursuing opportunities to contribute my technical skills and design sensibility to innovative architecture and interior projects across Canada. My approach combines precision with creativity—always aiming to enhance both the aesthetic and practical value of every space I help bring to life.
